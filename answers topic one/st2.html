SubTopic 2: World of ICTs

Qn9. Define the term ICT

ICT refers to a range of electronic tools and Technologies for storing, displaying and exchanging information and for communicating. OR
ICTs are all those technologies which can create, store, transmit, receive and send electronic information e.g. computers, radios, televisions, mobile phones, etc

Qn10. State any five areas where ICTs are in use today

  • Education
  • Business
  • Health
  • Security
  • Politics and Governance
  • Art, Leisure and Entertainment
  • Technical and Scientific uses

Qn11. Mention any five ways ICTs are used in the field of;
a. Education

  • Computer Assisted Instruction CAI
  • Computer Aided Learning (CAL)
  • Computer Aided Assessment (CAA)
  • School Administration and Management Systems. (SAMS)
  • Creating school websites for sharing information with the public
  • Students’ Report Cards are produced electronically
  • Production of error free timetables
  • E-learning and increased presence of virtual universities
  • Use of screen readers for the visually impaired
  • Use of simulation software to perform difficult or dangerous experiments in class

b. Business

  • Telecommuting
  • E-commerce
  • Computer Aided Design (CAD)
  • Electronic fund transfer
  • Use of Computers to manage transactions and Automatic Teller Machines ATMs for 24 hours banking
  • Advertisement

c. Health

  • Telemedicine
  • Tele-pharmacy
  • Managing and storing patients’ records electronically
  • Printing labels, allocating beds, make appointments, staff rotas, etc
  • Web sites for information on health care, treatments, conditions can be found on the internet
  • Computer Expert systems aid the monitoring and diagnosing Heart rate, blood pressure, etc.
  • Medical Training is facilitated by Simulation software and on-line data sources

d. Security

  • Monitoring security through cameras, Automatic number plate recognition, etc
  • Communication systems are widely used in the military to coordinate the personnel.
  • Some computer systems can detect temperatures and alarm in case of danger of fire outbreaks
  • Capturing data for Police National Computer Databases –, vehicle number plates, criminals’ fingerprints, etc.
  • Detect presence of illegal devices such as bombs
  • Computers are also used for controlling counter missiles

e. Politics and Governance

  • Paying government tax online through a government website
  • Online forms such as vehicle registration and passport forms
  • Advertising government tenders and Applying for government tenders
  • A maintained database of statistical information such as electoral register and census data can be availed online.
  • Use of electronic voting during elections
  • Government departments can use a computer based platform to get feedback from the citizens.

f. Communication

  • E-mail: Electronic Mail sent from one person to another using connected computers helps a lot in the area of communication.
  • Video Conferencing enables people in different locations to conduct meeting as if they are in the same location.
  • Computers enable people to send voice, image, text and data though telephones and mobile cell phones.
  • Social Networks such as Facebook, and Twitter enable people to stay in touch with their relatives, friends and interests.

g. Art, Leisure and Entertainment

  • Monitoring highway traffic
  • Computers are also used in Cinema halls and podiums for faster instant audio and video playback and presentation though projectors.
  • Computers are used in the entertainment industry to produce Music, Edit Video, CD burning, etc.
  • Computers are used for instant audio playback functions such as parties.

h. Technical and Scientific uses

  • Monitoring highway traffic
  • In Astronomy, Computers are essential tools to study the behavior of the complex systems in space as regards to their movements, interactions etc.
  • Aided Manufacture (CAM)
  • Computers perform Telescope pointing and tracking (including error correction), Camera operation, image download and storage, Image reduction (the measurement of the image), and Data processing.
  • Computers are used to tell schedules of water vessels, train, buses to their respective stations.
  • Computers are used very extensively in design of roads.
