SubTopic 3:Implications of using ICTs

Qn12. State any five positive implications of using ICT to the society

  • Computers have Created and widened employment opportunities
  • Improved education and research by simplifying teaching and learning.
  • Improved entertainment and leisure through computer games, music, etc for people to refresh and make-up.
  • Improved communication and collaboration through computer networks.
  • Improved health services where computers facilitate recording, monitoring, and diagnosis for patients.
  • Improved security through computer managed gates and monitoring of commercial and domestic premises, detecting and controlling crime by police.
  • Reduced production time and manufacturing processes through computer aided manufacturing and computer aided designing
  • Improved customer services delivery and care
  • Improved data and document production, storage and manipulation.

Qn15. Explain any five health concerns due to prolonged use of computers

  • People who spend their workday using the computer sometimes complain of lower back pain, muscle fatigue, and emotional fatigue.
  • A repetitive strain injury (RSI) is an injury or disorder of the muscles, nerves, tendons, ligaments, and joints. Computer-related RSIs include tendonitis and carpal tunnel syndrome.
    1. Tendonitis is inflammation of a tendon due to some repeated motion or stress on that tendon.
    2. Carpal tunnel syndrome (CTS) is inflammation of the nerve that connects the forearm to the palm of the wrist.
  • Eye strain and CVS; Computer Vision Syndrome (CVS)
