SubTopic 2: Secure Laboratory Environment

Qn11. Suggest two ways through which good air circulation can be achieved in a computer laboratory.

  • Having enough ventilation points
  • Installing an air conditioning system

Qn12. Suggest two reasons why standard furniture must be provided for a computer laboratory.

  • to avoid poor posture which may lead to strain injury.
  • The furniture (desk/table) where the computer system is placed should be stable to avoid the computer from falling which may lead to it's damage

Qn13. Mention five ways through which computers can be kept safe.

  • Physical Security Measures
    • Burglar proofing
    • Fit strong locks, doors, windows and roofing
    • Employ security guards
    • Set up alarms to alert
    • Use system locks
    • Electronic locking mechanism with keys, swipe cards, finger print recognition
    • CCTV Cameras to keep watch over computer systems and centers
    • Installing Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning (HVAC)
  • Electric Power Security
    • Do safe well insulated cabling.
    • Keep the circuits for computer systems separate from all other equipment such as printers, copiers and coffee makers
    • Plug each computer into a surge protector or UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply Unit)or power stablizers).
    • Lay cables along the walls in trunks
    • install lightening conductors
    • Minimize Electrical noise / interferences in the computer environment.
    • Installing Gaseous fire extinguishers
  • Software security measures
    • Computers should have updated antivirus software
    • Remote administration software should be considered
    • Assigning unique authorised log-in for authentication before granting network access etc

Qn14. Briefly explain the role of the following in the computer lab

  1. Burglar proofs: to prevent unauthorised entry into the lab
  2. A woollen carpet: to absorb dust and/or to act as a shock absorber for lighter devices
  3. Air Conditioner: to regulate temperature
  4. First Aid kit: helps the users to provide first aid treatment to others incase of an accident

Qn15. Mention any two roles of UPS in the lab

  • To stablise the power entering the computer
  • To act as a temporary backup power source
