Qn24. Suggest the procedure for cleaning the system case

  1. Gather your supplies. You will need a screwdriver, compressed air (either in a can or via a blower), a small vacuum cleaner / blower that can fit in narrow crevices, a toothbrush, and 99% isopropyl alcohol liquid
  2. Power down the computer and unplug all of the peripherals
  3. Setup an area where you can blow dust. A workbench near the window is a good choice.
  4. Unscrew and open the case side panel. NB: Set the screws aside so that you don't lose them
  5. Do an initial vacuum cleaning to suck up the large bits of dust from the components.
  6. Use the compressed air to blow dust out of crevices. Take your can of compressed air or air compressor and blow out the hard to reach crevices.
  7. You may remove some components for deep cleaning.
