SubTopic 1: Computer Literacy

Qn1. Define the term Computer literacy

Computer literacy is the knowledge and the ability to use computers and related technology efficiently.

Qn2. List any five skills possessed by a computer literate

  • Turning on the computer and log in, opening and creating folders and files,
  • Word processing, Spreadsheets skills,
  • Browser basics (Internet and Email),
  • Basic hardware terminology,
  • Virus and security awareness.

Qn5. Define the term computer laboratory

A computer laboratory is a special room set aside for safe installation and use of computers and other computer equipment.
