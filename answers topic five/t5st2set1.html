Qn27. Define a pixel

A pixel is a single point in an electronic image. OR A smallest dot that can be represented on a computer screen

Qn28. Define a screen resolution

Refers to the clearness or sharpness of a display screen OR In computers, resolution is the number of pixels (individual points of color) contained on a display monitor, expressed in terms of the number of pixels on the horizontal axis and the number on the vertical axis
